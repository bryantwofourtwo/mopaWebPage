MOPA LLC

One stop shop for Custom Networks, Custom A/V Solutions and Web Development needs

Mount Olympus Pro Audio, LLC opened their doors in 1997 installing Commercial and Residential Audio/Video. In 2001 Mount Olympus Pro Audio, LLC added installation and maintenance of Fire Station Alert Systems and Radios. The name Mount Olympus Pro Audio was shortened to MOPA, LLC over the years and has remained a recognized and respected name in the Audio/Video Installation industry.

Today MOPA is a well-established, full-service local Low Voltage Installation Company. We provide 24-hour emergency support to contracted customers. MOPA is proud to provide Installation, Sales, and Service of: Public Address Systems, Background Music Systems, Television Installs, Security Cameras, Business IT Support, Network and Networking Equipment, 2-Way Radios and Equipment, Bi-Directional Amplifiers, Fire Station Alert Systems (The system that notifies a fire station there is a call from 911 Dispatch not to be confused with Fire Alarm Systems).
